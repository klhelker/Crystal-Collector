Crystal Collector!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are four crystals above. The value of the crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. By clicking on one you will add a specific amount to your to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number on the screen. Sadly, you lose the game if you go over. For each new game the values of the crystals change!
